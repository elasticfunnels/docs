ElasticFunnels integrates with external call center providers to automatically push data for abandoned cart recovery, sales follow-ups, subscription dunning escalation, and cancellation routing.

<Info>
These are **outbound data pushes** to third-party providers. If you want to run your **own** call center with agent tools, phone orders, and commission tracking, see the [Call Center Suite](/callcenter/overview).
</Info>

## Supported Providers

  <Card title="Committed Coaches" icon="users">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Logicall" icon="phone">

  <Card title="Nuyu" icon="phone-volume">
    Sales and subscription management. Phone number is not required for sales.
  </Card>

## What Each Provider Supports

| Event Type | HelpGrid | Logicall | SalesBound | Committed Coaches | Nuyu |
|------------|----------|----------|------------|-------------------|------|
| **Abandoned carts** | Yes | Yes | — | — | — |
| **New sales** | — | — | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| **Failed payments** | — | — | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| **Subscription failed** | — | — | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| **Subscription cancel** | — | — | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| **Subscription winback** | — | — | Yes | Yes | Yes |

<Info>
**Committed Coaches** and **Nuyu** are purchase-endpoint providers — subscription events are sent via their sales webhook, not an abandon webhook. **Nuyu** is outbound-only (no incoming postbacks from the provider back to ElasticFunnels). **HelpGrid** and **Logicall** only handle abandoned cart data.
</Info>

## Common Use Cases

### Abandoned Cart Recovery

When a customer enters their email but doesn't complete the purchase, their data is sent to HelpGrid or Logicall. A call center agent can then reach out to help them complete the sale.

### New Order Follow-Up

After a successful purchase, order data is sent to SalesBound, Committed Coaches, or Nuyu. Ideal for high-ticket offers where a "welcome call" or "upsell call" is part of the flow.

### Subscription Dunning Escalation

When a subscription renewal fails and the retry count reaches your configured threshold, the failed payment data is sent to providers with subscription support. Agents can call the customer to update their payment method.

### Cancellation Routing

When a subscription is canceled, the data can be sent to providers for a retention call attempt.

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create the integration">
    Go to **Settings** → **Integrations**. Choose your provider and enter the **Webhook URL** or **API Keys** provided by your call center account manager. Save.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure merchant filtering">
    Set the integration to listen to **All Merchants** or a specific **Whitelist**. Use the whitelist if you have different call centers for different products or regions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable subscription escalation (optional)">
    Go to **Settings** → **Merchants**, edit your merchant, and open the **Email** tab. In **Dunning Configuration**, set **Callcenter Escalation After Retry #** and enable **Send to Callcenter on Cancel** if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test the connection">
    Use the **Test Webhook** button (available for most providers) to send a sample payload and verify the connection is active.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Always verify your Webhook URL with a test before going live to ensure your data is being captured correctly.
</Tip>

## Monitoring

You can see all outbound postbacks to your call centers in the **Logs** section of your dashboard. If a postback fails, the system logs the error response from the provider so you can troubleshoot.
